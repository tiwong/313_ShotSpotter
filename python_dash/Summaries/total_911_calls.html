Total Gunshot Calls

This chart represents the total amount of 911 calls of distinct types in a given Scout Car Area (SCA). SCAs are subdivisions of precincts to which officers can be assigned for patrol. Each type of SCA's total 911 calls are split into three types: Gunshots In Progress (SHOTSIP), Gunshots Just Happened (SHOTSJH), and Gunshots Detected by ShotSpotter (SHOTSPT).
