Scout Car Areas (SCA)

These charts represent the number of 911 calls for each three types over time for each SCA in a given precinct. Each type of SCA's total 911 calls are split into three types: Gunshots In Progress (SHOTSIP), Gunshots Just Happened (SHOTSJH), and Gunshots Detected by ShotSpotter (SHOTSPT). Because the nature of this research is investigating ShotSpotter, the time frames are limited to ShotSpotter involvement, if the given SCA has a ShotSpotter.
