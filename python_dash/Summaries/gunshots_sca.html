Total Number of Gunshots per SCA

Detroit's 11 police precincts are subdivided into 131 police scout car areas (SCA) that officers are assigned to patrol. The map below displays the total amount of gunshot-related 911 calls the DPD has received for each SCA from 2016 to the present. Note that in particular, some SCAs in the 9th precinct, where ShotSpotter was initially rolled out and continues to stay, have higher levels of gunshot-related 911 calls, moreso than some SCAs in the 8th precinct, another location where ShotSpotter was initially implemented.
